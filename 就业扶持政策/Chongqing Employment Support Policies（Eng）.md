## Employment Support Policies for College Graduates in Chongqing
### (I) Rental and Accommodation Security Policies
#### Municipal Overall Planning Accommodation Security Policies
1.  College graduates coming to Chongqing for job hunting for the first time can stay in the Youth Talent Station for free for a maximum of 14 days; young people such as college graduates employed in Chongqing can apply for public rental housing or affordable rental housing in accordance with regulations.
2.  Chongqing has launched a pilot public rental housing security service for fresh college graduates. Full-time fresh graduates from general higher education institutions who have graduated for less than 2 years, and full-time graduates who are about to graduate in the current year from general higher education institutions, who have no urban housing in the central urban area of Chongqing, can apply for the pilot housing, with a 1-year rental transition period. During the transition period, rental is only required to meet the housing-free condition, and after the transition period, the application will be reviewed in accordance with the conventional access conditions for public rental housing.
3.  Eligible fresh college graduates renting pilot public rental housing can enjoy 1 month of rent-free benefits; those who achieve employment or entrepreneurship in the central urban area during the transition period, provide labor contracts/business licenses and pay social insurance in accordance with the law, can apply for an additional 2 months of rent-free benefits, with a cumulative maximum of 3 months of rent-free benefits.
4.  All districts and counties are encouraged to provide monthly settlement subsidies to young talents introduced in their jurisdictions for a maximum of 3 years. The municipal finance provides a subsidy of 50% of the actual amount issued by the districts and counties, with a maximum subsidy of 50,000 yuan per person for doctoral degree holders and senior professional titles, and a maximum of 25,000 yuan per person for master's degree holders and intermediate professional titles.

#### District-specific Special Rental and Settlement Subsidy Policies
1.  Western Science City Chongqing High-tech Zone provides rental and housing purchase subsidies for eligible "Xinfeng Talents":
    - **Housing Purchase Subsidy**: For "Xinfeng Talents" who are working full-time in Chongqing for the first time, have signed a labor/employment contract of more than 3 years with employers within the directly administered park of Chongqing High-tech Zone, and purchase their first apartment within the directly administered park, a housing purchase subsidy of 120,000 yuan, 70,000 yuan and 20,000 yuan will be granted respectively for doctoral degree holders (senior professional titles or senior technicians), master's degree holders in urgently needed majors, and bachelor's degree holders in urgently needed majors from national "Double First-Class" universities or overseas universities ranked top 100 in the QS World University Rankings recognized by the Ministry of Education. All subsidies are issued in 3 years at a ratio of 30%, 30% and 40%. The housing purchase subsidy and settlement subsidy cannot be enjoyed repeatedly.
    - **Rental Subsidy**: For doctoral degree holders who have no self-owned property right housing within the directly administered park and rent talent housing, 50% of the monthly rent payable will be subsidized, with a maximum of 1,000 yuan per month. For master's degree holders in urgently needed majors and bachelor's degree holders in urgently needed majors from the corresponding universities who rent talent housing, 30% of the monthly rent payable will be subsidized, with a maximum of 600 yuan per month. The cumulative enjoyment period shall not exceed 2 years.
2.  Liangjiang New Area provides graduated settlement subsidies for fresh graduates who are working full-time in Chongqing for the first time, have signed a labor contract of more than 3 years with employers in the district, and pay social insurance in accordance with regulations: eligible doctoral degree holders can receive a maximum settlement subsidy of 100,000 yuan, master's degree holders can receive a one-off settlement subsidy of 20,000 yuan, and bachelor's degree holders can receive a one-off settlement subsidy of 10,000 yuan. Those who purchase houses in Longsheng New Town can enjoy additional corresponding graduated housing purchase subsidies.
3.  Hechuan District provides 1-7 days of free stay in 3 "Youth Talent Stations" for college graduates coming to Hechuan for job hunting; eligible young people can have priority to stay in talent apartments, and enjoy settlement subsidies or housing purchase subsidies. At the same time, a special discount of "Youth Preferential Rooms" in 15 hotels is launched.

### (II) Special Talent Subsidy Policies
#### Subsidies Available for Individual Graduates
1.  **One-off Job Hunting Subsidy**: For college graduates in the graduation academic year from various universities in Chongqing, who meet any of the following conditions and have the willingness to find employment or start a business: low-income families, zero-employment families, families under the monitoring system for poverty prevention, persons in extreme poverty, persons with disabilities, and those who have received national student loans. The subsidy standard is **800 yuan per person, limited to one time per person**, and no repeated enjoyment for multiple identities.
2.  **One-off Entrepreneurship Subsidy**: For college graduates within 5 years of graduation, who have established a small and micro enterprise or individual industrial and commercial household for the first time in Chongqing, have been in normal operation for more than 1 year and less than 3 years, have recruited workers and signed a labor contract of more than 1 year, and have paid social insurance for employees for more than 3 months in accordance with regulations, a subsidy of 2,000 yuan will be given for each person employed, with a **maximum of 8,000 yuan per household**. Eligible business entities can only enjoy the subsidy once.
3.  **Employment Subsidy for Shortage Positions**: College graduates under 35 years old who are employed in shortage positions in manufacturing and producer service enterprises in Chongqing, have continuously paid social insurance premiums for more than 1 year and are still insured, can apply for a **one-off employment subsidy of 6,000 yuan per person**. The same person can only enjoy it once.
4.  **Transportation, Accommodation and Food Subsidy**: For graduates from non-local universities in the graduation year who are employed in shortage positions of enterprises in Chongqing and have insured, can apply for a **one-off transportation, accommodation and food subsidy of 500 yuan per person**. The same person can only enjoy it once.
5.  **One-off Flexible Employment Subsidy**: Unemployed college graduates within 5 years of graduation, who have achieved the specified type of flexible employment in Chongqing, have continuously paid urban employee basic endowment insurance premiums as an individual for more than 1 year and are still insured, can apply for a **one-off flexible employment subsidy of 6,000 yuan per person**, which cannot be enjoyed repeatedly with the social insurance subsidy for flexible employment.
6.  **Employment Internship Subsidy**: Chongqing collects no less than 120,000 internship positions every year. College graduates can participate in employment internships for a maximum of 12 months at recognized internship bases. For the bases organizing internships, an employment internship subsidy is given at the standard of **1,300 yuan per person per month**. For those with a retention rate of more than 50%, the subsidy standard is increased to **1,500 yuan per person per month**.
7.  **Special Subsidy for the "Three Supports and One Assistance" Program**: Participants in Chongqing's "Three Supports and One Assistance" Program who have served in their positions for more than 6 months can enjoy a **one-off settlement subsidy of 3,000 yuan**. At the same time, they can enjoy work and living subsidies, social insurance, housing provident fund, allowances for remote and border areas, and township work subsidies in accordance with regulations.

#### Employment Subsidies for Employing Units
1.  **One-off Job Expansion Subsidy**: Various enterprises and social organizations that newly recruit graduates in the graduation year, unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation registered as unemployed, and registered unemployed youths aged 16-24, sign labor contracts with them, and continuously pay relevant social insurance premiums for more than 3 months in accordance with regulations and are still paying, can enjoy a one-off job expansion subsidy at the standard of **1,500 yuan per person**.
2.  **One-off Employment Absorption Subsidy**: Small and micro enterprises that newly recruit unemployed college graduates within 5 years of graduation registered as unemployed, have continuously paid social insurance premiums for more than 1 year for them and are still insured, can enjoy a one-off employment absorption subsidy at the standard of **5,000 yuan per person**, which cannot be enjoyed repeatedly with the unit social insurance subsidy for college graduates.
3.  **Tax and Fee Reduction for Employment Absorption**: For enterprises that recruit college graduates who have been registered as unemployed for more than 6 months at the public employment service agency of the human resources and social security department and hold the *Employment and Entrepreneurship Certificate*, sign a labor contract of more than 1 year with them, and pay social insurance premiums in accordance with the law, from the month of signing the labor contract and paying social insurance, a fixed tax and fee deduction of **7,800 yuan per person per year** is granted within 3 years according to the actual number of recruited personnel. The deduction is applied sequentially to value-added tax, urban maintenance and construction tax, education surcharge, local education surcharge and corporate income tax.

### (III) Social Insurance Subsidy Policies
1.  **Social Insurance Subsidy for Small and Micro Enterprises Recruiting College Graduates**: Small and micro enterprises that newly recruit graduates in the graduation year and unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation registered as unemployed, have continuously paid social insurance premiums for more than 1 year for them and are still insured, will be given a social insurance subsidy at the standard of **10,000 yuan per person per year**, with a subsidy period of 1 year. The same person can only enjoy it once.
2.  **Social Insurance Subsidy for Flexible Employment of Unemployed College Graduates After Graduation**: Unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation, who have achieved the specified type of flexible employment in Chongqing, have continuously paid urban employee basic endowment insurance premiums as an individual for more than 1 year and are still insured, will be given a social insurance subsidy at the standard of **6,000 yuan per person per year**, with a maximum subsidy period of no more than 2 years.
