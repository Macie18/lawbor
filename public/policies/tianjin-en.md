# Summary of Tianjin's Five Social Insurances, Housing Fund and Related Social Security Policies
**Based on the official content of the Social Insurance Premiums Column of the State Taxation Administration Tianjin Municipal Taxation Bureau**
> Note: This summary is fully based on the currently effective policies of Tianjin Municipality. From November 1, 2020, all social insurance premiums for enterprise employees in Tianjin have been uniformly collected by the tax authorities, and all payment-related businesses can be handled through Tianjin Taxation channels.
---
## I. Five Social Insurances and Housing Fund
### (I) Contribution Rate Inquiry (Tianjin Exclusive Standards)
#### 1. Contribution Rates for Enterprise Employees
| Type of Insurance | Employer Contribution Rate | Employee Contribution Rate | Core Policy Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Basic Endowment Insurance | 16% | 8% | Unified standard for the whole city, uniformly collected by the tax authorities, with cumulative calculation of contribution years |
| Basic Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance) | 10.5% (including maternity insurance premiums) | 2%; Annual large-sum medical assistance fee for employees in 2025: 260 RMB per person | Maternity insurance is integrated with employee medical insurance, with no separate personal contribution item, and the insured can enjoy serious illness medical assistance benefits simultaneously |
| Unemployment Insurance | 0.50% | 0.50% | Unified standard for the whole city, implementing the benchmark rate of 1%, with the employer and employee each bearing 50%. The contribution years are calculated cumulatively, and the policy is extended to April 30, 2026 |
| Work-related Injury Insurance | 0.2%-1.9% (benchmark rates for 8 industry risk levels) | 0% | The phased fee reduction policy is continued in 2025, with a unified 20% phased reduction on the basis of the benchmark rate. Construction projects shall participate in insurance and pay contributions separately by project, with no employee contribution |
| Housing Provident Fund (Statutory) | 5%-12% (same proportion as employee) | 5%-12% (same proportion as employer) | Contributing entities may independently choose the contribution rate within the range. The same unit shall implement the same contribution rate, and the individual contribution rate shall not be lower than the employer's contribution rate |
| Supplementary Housing Provident Fund | 1%-15% (same proportion as employee) | 1%-15% (same proportion as employer) | Non-mandatory contribution, voluntarily opened by the employer. The total contribution rate of the employer and individual shall not exceed 30% |

#### 2. Contribution Rates for Flexibly Employed Persons
- Employee Endowment Insurance: Contribution rate of 20%, fully borne by the individual
- Employee Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance): Contribution rate of 8.5%, with an additional annual large-sum medical assistance fee to be paid, fully borne by the individual
- Optional Insurance: Housing Provident Fund, which can be voluntarily contributed at a rate of 10%-24%, with the full monthly contribution borne by the individual. Those who have made continuous contributions for 6 months can enjoy the same provident fund loan benefits as enterprise employees
- Contribution Base: Can be independently selected between the upper and lower limits of the social insurance contribution base of Tianjin in the current year (2025 upper limit: 25620 RMB/month, lower limit: 5124 RMB/month)

#### 3. Official Inquiry Channels
Official website of the State Taxation Administration Tianjin Municipal Taxation Bureau (Social Insurance Premiums Column), official website of Tianjin Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, official website of Tianjin Municipal Healthcare Security Bureau, official website of Tianjin Municipal Housing Provident Fund Management Center, Jinxinban APP, Palm 12333 APP.

### (II) Impacts of Payment Interruption
#### 1. Core Impacts of Interruption of the Five Social Insurances
| Insurance | Specific Impacts of Payment Interruption |
| :--- | :--- |
| Endowment Insurance | In accordance with the *Social Insurance Law of the People's Republic of China* and *Regulations of Tianjin Municipality on Endowment Insurance for Urban Enterprise Employees*: Individuals participating in the basic endowment insurance shall receive a monthly basic pension upon reaching the statutory retirement age, provided that the cumulative contribution period is at least 15 years. Interruption of social insurance contributions does not affect the cumulative calculation of the endowment insurance contribution period; the contribution years before and after the interruption are calculated cumulatively, and the balance in the endowment insurance individual account continues to accumulate. However, pension calculation follows the principle of "more contributions, more benefits; longer contributions, more benefits". The longer the payment interruption period, the shorter the cumulative contribution years, the relatively less the accumulated amount in the individual account, and the lower the pension received after retirement. If the cumulative contribution period is less than 15 years upon reaching the statutory retirement age, the payment shall be extended further. Only unpaid social insurance premiums during the period of employment with an employer can be applied for supplementary payment by the employer; individuals shall not make supplementary payment for historical interruption periods on their own. |
| Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance) | From the month following the payment interruption, the insured shall cease to enjoy the medical insurance pooling reimbursement benefits, while the balance in the individual account can be used normally. For flexibly employed persons whose payment is interrupted for more than 3 months, the following provisions shall be implemented from June 1, 2026: Those who have paid basic medical insurance continuously for two years or more (including supplementary payment) with interruption ≤ 3 months shall have no benefit waiting period after resuming payment; those who do not meet the above conditions shall have a 3-month benefit waiting period after resuming payment, during which no pooling fund reimbursement benefits are available. To apply for maternity allowance and maternity medical benefits, the insured must have paid employee medical insurance (including maternity insurance) continuously for 6 months before childbirth, with normal insurance participation in the month of childbirth/termination of pregnancy; payment interruption will directly affect the application eligibility. Payment interruption will affect the continuous contribution period and the actual local payment period of medical insurance. Tianjin stipulates that the cumulative contribution period of employee medical insurance must be at least 25 years for males and 20 years for females, with an actual local payment period of at least 5 years, to enjoy lifetime medical insurance benefits after retirement. |
| Unemployment Insurance | The contribution period is calculated cumulatively. Payment interruption does not affect the accumulated contribution period, but the core requirement for receiving unemployment benefits is a cumulative contribution of at least 1 year before unemployment. If re-employment leads to unemployment again, the payment period shall be recalculated, and the uncollected period from the previous time may be combined for calculation, with the maximum combined period not exceeding 24 months. |
| Work-related Injury Insurance | If a work-related accident occurs during the period of payment interruption, the work-related injury insurance fund shall not pay the relevant benefits, and all benefits shall be fully borne by the employer in accordance with the statutory standards in the *Several Provisions of Tianjin Municipality on Work-related Injury Insurance*. After the employer makes supplementary payment of work-related injury insurance premiums, the newly occurred work-related injury benefits shall be paid by the work-related injury insurance fund in accordance with regulations. |
| Additional Impacts | There are strict requirements on continuous social insurance contributions for policies such as house purchase, points-based household registration, residence permit points, and license plate bidding in Tianjin. Among them, non-local registered residents purchasing housing need to provide a certificate of continuous social insurance/individual income tax payment for more than 2 years within 3 years, with supplementary payment not recognized. Payment interruption will directly affect the application for relevant qualifications. |

#### 2. Core Impacts of Housing Provident Fund Payment Interruption
- To apply for a housing provident fund loan, continuous contributions for at least 6 months before the application are required. Payment interruption of 1 month or more will reset the continuous contribution period to zero, making the applicant ineligible for a provident fund loan;
- The account will be properly sealed during the interruption period, which does not affect the existing account balance. Withdrawal can be processed normally for those who meet specific conditions (e.g., non-local registered persons with the account sealed for more than 6 months, house purchase, house rental, etc.);
- For already disbursed provident fund loans, payment interruption does not affect the normal repayment, as long as the loan is repaid in full and on time.

### (III) Transfer and Continuity Process
#### 1. Inter-provincial/Inter-pooling Area Transfer and Continuity of Social Insurance
Social insurance premiums in Tianjin are uniformly collected by the tax authorities. The core handling entities for transfer and continuity are the social insurance/medical insurance handling agencies, the tax authorities are responsible for the verification and confirmation of payment records. The whole process can be handled online without travel between the two places.
- **Basic Endowment Insurance Transfer**
  - Required Materials:
    1. Resident ID card or Social Security Card (1 original copy);
    2. *Benefit Receipt Location Confirmation Form* or materials approved by the organization, human resources and social security administrative department at or above the county level for transfer (1 original copy);
    3. Household register of the applicant (provided if the male is over 50 years old and the female is over 40 years old when the applicant first participates in insurance in the city, 1 original copy);
    4. Endowment Insurance Participation and Payment Certificate for Unemployed Military Spouses Accompanying the Army (provided for the transfer of endowment insurance for unemployed military spouses accompanying the army, 1 original copy).
  - Handling Methods:
    1. On-site handling at the handling counter;
    2. Online application handling: The insured may apply for endowment insurance transfer through the National Social Insurance Public Service Platform, Human Resources and Social Security Government Affairs Service Platform, or mobile APPs with electronic social security card functions such as Palm 12333, WeChat Mini Program of the State Council Client, Alipay, WeChat, and Tianjin Human Resources and Social Security.
- **Medical Insurance Transfer**
  1. For insured persons moving to the city from other provinces: Insured employees with a unit may apply through the unit, and flexibly employed persons, urban and rural residents and other insured persons shall apply individually. When applying, they may submit the application offline at the window of the local handling agency, or online through the National Medical Security Information Platform, Tianjin Medical Security Online Service Hall, and Jin Yibao APP.
  2. For insured persons moving from the city to other provinces: The insured shall submit an application to the handling agency of the other province in accordance with the relevant regulations of the other province, or may submit an application to the local handling agency online.
- **Unemployment Insurance Transfer**
  1. After stopping the insurance in Tianjin, the applicant may submit a transfer application online through the National Social Insurance Public Service Platform, or submit an offline application to the unemployment insurance handling agency at the transfer-in location;
  2. After acceptance, the transfer-in location shall send a contact letter, the Tianjin handling agency shall transfer the corresponding funds and issue the payment record. For those with a contribution period of less than 1 year, only the relationship shall be transferred without fund transfer;
  3. After the transfer-in location completes the process, the contribution years shall be accumulated and merged, and the unemployment benefit standard shall be verified in accordance with the regulations of the transfer-in location.

#### 2. Transfer and Continuity of Housing Provident Fund
1. **Off-site Transfer into Tianjin**: After opening a provident fund account in Tianjin and making normal contributions, submit an off-site transfer and continuity application through the National Housing Provident Fund Mini Program, Jinxinban APP, or the official website of Tianjin Municipal Housing Provident Fund Management Center. The Tianjin Provident Fund Center will complete the account merger with the transfer-out location after acceptance, with the whole process completed within 11 working days;
2. **Transfer from Tianjin to Off-site**: After sealing the provident fund account in Tianjin, submit a transfer application to the provident fund center at the transfer-in location, and handle it in accordance with the process of the transfer-in location. The Tianjin Provident Fund Center will cooperate in the balance transfer and information transfer, with the whole process completed within 11 working days.

### (IV) Usage Guide (Medical Treatment, Provident Fund Loan)
#### 1. Medical Insurance Usage Guide
- **Prerequisite for Benefit Enjoyment**: Normal insured contribution with no benefit waiting period due to payment interruption, then the insured can enjoy Tianjin employee medical insurance benefits. During the period of receiving unemployment insurance benefits, the unemployment insurance fund shall pay the employee basic medical insurance (including maternity insurance) premiums and large-sum medical assistance fee on behalf of the insured, with no personal contribution, and the insured shall enjoy employee medical insurance benefits normally.
- **Outpatient Usage**
  1. Deductible: Annual deductible of 800 RMB for working employees, 700 RMB for retirees (under 70 years old), 650 RMB for retirees (70 years old and above);
  2. Reimbursement Rate: Above the deductible, 75% reimbursement at primary hospitals, 65% at secondary hospitals, 55% at tertiary hospitals;
  3. Settlement Method: Direct card settlement at designated medical institutions in Tianjin with a social security card/medical insurance electronic voucher, and the individual only pays the self-paid portion. After completing the off-site medical treatment filing, direct settlement can be made at designated medical institutions nationwide.
- **Inpatient Usage**
  1. Deductible Standard
  | Number of Hospitalizations | Primary Hospital | Secondary Hospital | Tertiary Hospital |
  | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
  | First Hospitalization | 800 RMB | 1100 RMB | 1700 RMB |
  | Second Hospitalization | 270 RMB | 350 RMB | 500 RMB |
  2. Reimbursement Payment Standard
  | Insured Type | Expense Range | Reimbursement Rate |
  | :--- | :--- | :--- |
  | Working Employees | Below 120,000 RMB (inclusive) | 85% |
  | Working Employees | 120,000 RMB - 450,000 RMB (inclusive) | 80% |
  | Retired Employees | Below 180,000 RMB (inclusive) | 90% |
  | Retired Employees | 180,000 RMB - 450,000 RMB (inclusive) | 80% |
- **Maternity Insurance Benefits**: Integrated into employee medical insurance. The maternity allowance is calculated as the average monthly wage of the employer in the previous year ÷ 30 × the number of days of maternity leave allowance the employee is entitled to, and paid by the medical insurance fund. Prenatal examination, childbirth, family planning surgery and other maternity medical expenses in line with the regulations shall be fully reimbursed at the prescribed proportion.

#### 2. Housing Provident Fund Loan Usage Guide
- **Application Prerequisites**
  1. Continuous housing provident fund contributions for at least 6 months before the application, with the account in normal contribution status;
  2. Compliance with Tianjin's housing purchase restriction policies, purchasing owner-occupied housing in the city;
  3. No outstanding provident fund loans, and personal credit meets the requirements;
  4. Not exceeding the statutory retirement age (maximum 65 years old).
- **Limit and Interest Rate**
  1. **Basic Maximum Limit**
     - First Home: 1.2 million RMB;
     - Second Home: 1 million RMB;
     - Families with multiple children (raising two or more children with at least one minor): 20% increase (1.44 million RMB for first home, 1.2 million RMB for second home).
  2. **Loan Interest Rate**
     - First Home: Annual interest rate of 2.10% for 5 years or less, 2.60% for more than 5 years;
     - Second Home: Annual interest rate of no less than 2.525% for 5 years or less, no less than 3.075% for more than 5 years.
  3. **Down Payment Ratio**: Minimum down payment of 20% for both first and second homes (loan amount not exceeding 80% of the house price).

## II. Work-related Injury Handling
### (I) Determination Standards and Process
#### 1. Statutory Determination Standards
- **Circumstances That Shall Be Determined as Work-related Injury**
  1. Suffering an accidental injury due to work reasons during working hours and in the workplace;
  2. Suffering an accidental injury from preparatory or finishing work related to the job in the workplace before or after working hours;
  3. Suffering accidental injury such as violence due to performing work duties during working hours and in the workplace;
  4. Suffering from an occupational disease;
  5. Suffering injury or missing in an accident due to work reasons during work-related travel;
  6. Suffering injury from a traffic accident, urban rail transit, passenger ferry, or train accident for which the person is not primarily responsible, on the way to or from work;
  7. Other circumstances that shall be determined as work-related injury in accordance with laws and administrative regulations.
- **Circumstances That Shall Be Deemed as Work-related Injury**
  1. Sudden illness leading to death or death after failed rescue within 48 hours during working hours and at the work post;
  2. Suffering injury in activities to safeguard national interests or public interests such as emergency rescue and disaster relief;
  3. Employees who served in the army, became disabled due to war or duty, obtained a Revolutionary Disabled Soldier Certificate, and have a recurrence of the old injury after joining the employer.
- **Circumstances That Shall Not Be Determined or Deemed as Work-related Injury**: Intentional crime; intoxication or drug use; self-harm or suicide.
- **Supplementary Insurance Coverage**: Employees over the statutory retirement age under 65 years old and interns may be separately covered by work-related injury insurance by the employer, and enjoy work-related injury benefits in accordance with regulations if a work-related injury occurs after insurance participation. Mobile employees of construction projects shall participate in insurance by project and be included in the coverage of work-related injury insurance.

#### 2. Determination and Handling Process
1. **Application Time Limit**: The employer shall submit an application for work-related injury determination to the District Human Resources and Social Security Bureau (HRSSB) at the place of registration of the unit within 30 days from the date of the accidental injury or the date of diagnosis of occupational disease. If the employer fails to apply on time, the employee/close relatives/trade union may directly submit the application within 1 year.
2. **Application Materials**: Work-related injury determination application form, proof of labor relationship, medical diagnosis certificate/occupational disease diagnosis certificate, and relevant proof materials of the accidental injury.
3. **Acceptance and Review**: The District HRSSB shall make an acceptance decision within 15 days, and inform the applicant of the supplements to be made in one go if the materials are incomplete. After acceptance, it may conduct investigation and verification of the accidental injury, and the relevant entities and individuals shall cooperate.
4. **Determination Decision**: A work-related injury determination decision shall be made within 60 days from the date of acceptance, issuing the *Decision on Determination of Work-related Injury* or *Decision on Rejection of Work-related Injury Determination*, which shall be served in writing to the employer and the employee.
5. **Labor Capacity Appraisal**: After the work-related injury is determined, if the employee has a disability affecting labor capacity after the injury is stable, he/she shall apply to the Tianjin Municipal Labor Capacity Appraisal Committee for appraisal of disability grade and self-care ability grade. The appraisal conclusion shall be made within 60 days, and may be extended for 30 days if necessary.

### (II) Calculation of Compensation Items
Compensation items are divided into two categories: **paid by the work-related injury insurance fund** and **paid by the employer**. For those not insured in accordance with the law, all benefits shall be fully borne by the employer in accordance with the standard.

#### 1. Items Paid by the Work-related Injury Insurance Fund and Calculation Standards
| Compensation Item | Calculation Standard |
| :--- | :--- |
| Work-related Injury Medical/Rehabilitation Expenses | Full reimbursement for expenses in line with the work-related injury insurance diagnosis and treatment, medicine, and inpatient service standards |
| Inpatient Food Allowance | Current standard in Tianjin: 30 RMB per day |
| Transportation, Accommodation and Food Expenses for Off-site Medical Treatment | Transportation expenses: Reimbursed according to actual expenses (long-distance bus, hard seat/soft berth of train, third class cabin of ship and below); Accommodation fee: Maximum 150 RMB per day; Food allowance: 50 RMB per day |
| Disability Assistive Device Fees | Full reimbursement for those in line with national and Tianjin standards |
| Life Care Fees | After appraisal and confirmation, calculated based on the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Tianjin: 50% for total inability to self-care, 40% for most inability to self-care, 30% for partial inability to self-care |
| Lump-sum Disability Benefit | Calculated as the average monthly contributory wage of the 12 months before the work-related injury × corresponding number of months: Level 1: 27 months, Level 2: 25 months, Level 3: 23 months, Level 4: 21 months, Level 5: 18 months, Level 6: 16 months, Level 7: 13 months, Level 8: 11 months, Level 9: 9 months, Level 10: 7 months |
| Disability Allowance for Level 1 to Level 4 | Paid monthly, calculated as a proportion of the employee's wage: Level 1: 90%, Level 2: 85%, Level 3: 80%, Level 4: 75%. If the actual amount of the disability allowance is lower than the minimum wage standard in Tianjin, the difference shall be made up by the work-related injury insurance fund. |
| Lump-sum Work-related Injury Medical Allowance | Paid upon dissolution/termination of labor relationship, calculated as the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Tianjin × corresponding number of months: Level 5: 12 months, Level 6: 10 months, Level 7: 8 months, Level 8: 6 months, Level 9: 4 months, Level 10: 2 months |
| Funeral Allowance (Fatal Work-related Injury) | Calculated as the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Tianjin × 6 months |
| Bereavement Pension for Dependent Relatives (Fatal Work-related Injury) | Calculated as a proportion of the employee's wage before death: 40% per month for the spouse, 30% per month for each other dependent relative, with an additional 10% per month for widowed elderly or orphans on the basis of the above standards. The total amount shall not exceed the employee's wage before death. |
| Lump-sum Fatal Work-related Injury Benefit (Fatal Work-related Injury) | Calculated as the per capita disposable income of urban residents nationwide in the previous year × 20 times |

#### 2. Items Paid by the Employer and Calculation Standards
| Compensation Item | Calculation Standard |
| :--- | :--- |
| Salary During Work Suspension Period | During the work suspension period, the original wage and welfare benefits shall remain unchanged and paid monthly. The period shall generally not exceed 12 months, and may be extended with approval for serious injuries, but the extension shall not exceed 12 months. |
| Disability Allowance for Level 5 and Level 6 | For those who retain the labor relationship and are difficult to arrange work, paid monthly, calculated as a proportion of the employee's wage: Level 5: 70%, Level 6: 60%. If it is lower than the minimum wage standard in Tianjin, the employer shall make up the difference. |
| Lump-sum Disability Employment Allowance | Paid upon dissolution/termination of labor relationship, calculated as the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Tianjin × corresponding number of months: Level 5: 18 months, Level 6: 15 months, Level 7: 12 months, Level 8: 9 months, Level 9: 6 months, Level 10: 3 months |
| Life Care Fees During Work Suspension Period | For injured employees who are unable to take care of themselves and need nursing during the work suspension period, the employer shall be responsible for the nursing. |

### (III) Dispute Resolution Channels
1. **Work-related Injury Determination Dispute**: If the employee or the employer is dissatisfied with the work-related injury determination decision or the rejection decision, he/she may apply for administrative reconsideration within 60 days from the date of receiving the decision; if dissatisfied with the reconsideration decision, he/she may file an administrative lawsuit within 15 days, or directly file an administrative lawsuit within 6 months from the date of receiving the decision.
2. **Work-related Injury Benefit Payment Dispute**: Disputes between the employee and the employer regarding work-related injury benefits, salary during work suspension period, disability employment allowance, etc., are labor disputes. The employee may apply for labor arbitration to the District Labor and Personnel Dispute Arbitration Committee at the place of registration of the employer or the place of performance of the labor contract within 1 year from the date of the dispute; if dissatisfied with the arbitration award, he/she may file a civil lawsuit with the people's court within 15 days.
3. **Other Disputes**: If dissatisfied with the benefits verified by the work-related injury insurance handling agency, the applicant may apply for administrative reconsideration or file an administrative lawsuit. If the employer fails to pay work-related injury insurance premiums in accordance with the law, a complaint and report may be filed with the State Taxation Administration Tianjin Municipal Taxation Bureau, which shall order the employer to make supplementary payment within a time limit.

## III. Unemployment Insurance
### (I) Eligibility Judgment for Collection
An applicant may apply for Tianjin unemployment insurance benefits if all of the following conditions are met at the same time:
1. The employer and the applicant have paid unemployment insurance premiums for a cumulative period of at least 1 year before unemployment, and Tianjin is the last payment place;
2. The employment is interrupted not due to the applicant's own will (including expiration of the labor contract, dissolution/dismissal by the employer, termination of the labor contract by the employee due to the employer's illegal employment, etc.);
3. The applicant has completed unemployment registration and has job-seeking requirements.

**Supplementary Rights and Interests**: During the period of receiving unemployment insurance benefits, the unemployment insurance fund shall pay the employee basic medical insurance (including maternity insurance) premiums and large-sum medical assistance fee on behalf of the insured, with no personal contribution, and the insured shall enjoy employee medical insurance benefits normally. Free job introduction, vocational training and other employment services are available; if the insured dies during the collection period, his/her survivors may apply for funeral allowance and pension.

**Circumstances Ineligible for Collection**: Re-employment; enlistment in active military service; emigration abroad; enjoying basic endowment insurance benefits; being imprisoned and executed for a crime; refusing to accept an appropriate job or training introduced by the public employment service agency without justifiable reasons.
---
### (II) Amount Calculation
#### 1. Payment Standards (effective from July 1, 2024, currently effective)
- Payment standard for the 1st-12th months: 1730 RMB/month
- Payment standard for the 13th-24th months: 1690 RMB/month

#### 2. Verification of Collection Period
- Cumulative payment of 1 year or more but less than 3 years: maximum collection period of 6 months;
- Cumulative payment of 3 years or more but less than 5 years: maximum collection period of 12 months;
- Cumulative payment of 5 years or more but less than 10 years: maximum collection period of 18 months;
- Cumulative payment of 10 years or more: maximum collection period of 24 months.

**Supplementary Rules**: If re-employment leads to unemployment again, the payment period shall be recalculated, and the uncollected period from the previous time may be combined for calculation, with the maximum combined period not exceeding 24 months.
---
### (III) Application Process
1. **Online Application** (full online processing, no offline materials required): Search for "Unemployment Insurance Benefit Application" through Jinxinban APP/Mini Program, "Palm 12333" APP, or the online service hall on the official website of Tianjin Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, check personal information and submit the application.
2. **Offline Application**: Bring your ID card and social security card to the District Employment Service Center or sub-district (town) Party and Mass Service Center/community affairs service center at the place of household registration/residence to complete the unemployment registration and application procedures on site.
3. **Review and Payment**: The handling agency shall complete the review and inform the result within 5 working days. If the application is approved, the unemployment insurance benefits shall be calculated from the date of unemployment registration and paid monthly to the applicant's social security card bank account or designated bank account. Normal payment can be made by completing the monthly employment status confirmation during the collection period.
