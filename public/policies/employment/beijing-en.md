## Employment Support Policies for College Graduates in Beijing
### (I) Rental and Accommodation Security Policies
#### District-specific Special Rental and Accommodation Subsidy Policies
1.  For fresh graduates who graduate between January and December 2026, hold a full-time bachelor's degree or above, are employed or self-employed by employers within Haidian District's "1+X+1" modern industrial system, pay social insurance in Haidian District, have no self-owned housing in Beijing for themselves and their family members, and have not enjoyed other affordable housing policies, Haidian District provides an **accommodation subsidy of 1,000 yuan per month for a maximum of 2 years**. For those who rent housing in the rental market of the 4 northern towns of Haidian and complete the lease contract filing, an additional accommodation reward of 500 yuan per month is available, **totaling up to 1,500 yuan per month**.
2.  Haidian District launches no less than 2,000 sets of fully furnished, ready-to-move-in youth apartments every year, allocated to young talents who have graduated within the past 3 years and are employed in Haidian, with a graduated rent discount for a maximum of 6 years: 40% off (60% of market rent) for the 1st-2nd years, 30% off (70% of market rent) for the 3rd-4th years, and 20% off (80% of market rent) for the 5th-6th years.
3.  Chaoyang District has launched a special preferential rental housing policy for domestic and international graduates who have obtained a full-time bachelor's degree or above after January 1, 2026. Unemployed graduates can enjoy 1 month of rent-free and deposit-free benefits; those employed in Chaoyang District can enjoy up to 3 months of rent-free and deposit-free benefits, with housing types including entire apartments, shared single rooms, dormitory beds and other categories.
4.  Mentougou District provides up to 7 days of free accommodation for talents coming to the district for job interviews; college students who have signed an internship agreement with enterprises registered and paying taxes in the district can get up to 3 months of rent-free accommodation. For college students employed by enterprises in the district, a graduated rent subsidy is provided: 80% of the rent for doctoral degree holders, 70% for master's degree holders, and 60% for bachelor's degree holders in the first year of employment; a unified 50% rent subsidy for all academic backgrounds starting from the second year.
5.  Changping District has launched the "Swift Talent Apartment", building a stepped security system of "7-day free trial period + 3-month zero-cost transition period + up to 3 years of 50% off accommodation period". It covers college students under 35 years old, graduates in the job-hunting period, and young talents employed in the district. The housing fully covers areas with concentrated young talents such as the Huitian Area and Changping Urban Area, and is fully furnished for immediate move-in.
6.  Beijing Economic and Technological Development Area (BDA) has launched a stepped housing security service for young talents under 35 years old (inclusive) with a full-time bachelor's degree or above, who are job hunting, interning, employed or starting a business in the zone: 7 days of free accommodation for job hunting, up to 3 months of free short-term rental for internships, and up to 3 years of preferential long-term rental for employment. Meanwhile, young entrepreneurial talents are included in the security system, and eligible entrepreneurial youths can enjoy up to 3 months of free accommodation at one time, followed by preferential rent within 3 years.
7.  Shunyi District provides up to 5 days of free accommodation for fresh graduates with full-time bachelor's degree or above from non-Beijing universities who come to job hunting with employers in the district; a special talent apartment allocation policy is provided for fresh graduates who come to the district for internships or employment.

### (II) Special Talent Subsidy Policies
#### Subsidies Available for Individual Graduates
1.  **One-off Job Hunting and Entrepreneurship Subsidy**: For full-time graduates in the graduation academic year from general higher education institutions and postgraduate training institutions in Beijing, who meet any of the following conditions and have the willingness to find employment or start a business (excluding定向 and commissioned training students): families receiving minimum living security for urban and rural residents, zero-employment families, families under the monitoring system for poverty prevention, persons with disabilities, those who received national student loans during the graduation academic year, and holders of the extreme poverty assistance and support certificate. The subsidy standard is **1,000 yuan per person, limited to one time per person**.
2.  **One-off First-time Entrepreneurship Subsidy**: For college graduates with Beijing household registration who are in the graduation year or within 2 years of graduation, who have established an enterprise, individual industrial and commercial household or other entrepreneurial organization for the first time in Beijing, serve as the legal representative or principal person in charge, have paid social insurance in the established organization for a cumulative total of 6 months, have operated normally and paid taxes in accordance with the law for more than 1 year since registration, and have good credit without being listed on the dishonest blacklist, can apply for a **one-off entrepreneurship subsidy of 8,000 yuan**. For each additional local registered labor force recruited by the entrepreneurial organization who has paid social insurance for a cumulative total of 6 months, an additional 1,000 yuan subsidy is granted, with the total subsidy for each entrepreneurial organization not exceeding 10,000 yuan.
3.  **Tuition Compensation and Student Loan Repayment for Grassroots Employment**: For college graduates who take up grassroots positions in Beijing, the tuition compensation and national student loan repayment policies shall be implemented in accordance with regulations.

#### Employment Subsidies for Employing Units
1.  **One-off Job Expansion Subsidy**: From January 1 to December 31, 2025, enterprises and social organizations in Beijing that recruit graduates in the graduation year and unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation, sign labor contracts, and pay unemployment, work-related injury, and employee endowment insurance premiums in full for more than 3 months in accordance with regulations, can enjoy a one-off job expansion subsidy at the standard of **1,000 yuan per person recruited**. The application period for enterprises and social organizations is extended to April 30, 2026.
2.  **Tax and Fee Reduction for Employment Absorption**: For enterprises that recruit college graduates who have been registered as unemployed for more than 6 months at the public employment service agency of the human resources and social security department and hold the *Employment and Entrepreneurship Certificate*, sign a labor contract of more than 1 year with them, and pay social insurance premiums in accordance with the law, from the month of signing the labor contract and paying social insurance, a fixed tax and fee deduction of **7,800 yuan per person per year** is granted within 3 years according to the actual number of recruited personnel. The deduction is applied sequentially to value-added tax, urban maintenance and construction tax, education surcharge, local education surcharge and corporate income tax.

### (III) Employment and Entrepreneurship Support Policies
1.  **Relaxation of Fresh Graduate Identity Definition**: For positions recruiting "fresh college graduates" by Beijing's government agencies, public institutions and local state-owned enterprises, applications are open to college graduates within 2 years of the graduation year on the diploma (including the graduation year itself), with no restrictions on work experience or social insurance payment. The graduation time is subject to the graduation year on the graduate's diploma.
2.  **Tax and Fee Reduction for Entrepreneurship and Operation**: For college graduates in the graduation year holding the *Employment and Entrepreneurship Certificate* (marked with "self-employment tax policy for the graduation year") who engage in individual business operations, within 3 years starting from the month of completing the individual industrial and commercial household registration, a quota of **24,000 yuan per household per year** is applied to sequentially deduct the value-added tax, urban maintenance and construction tax, education surcharge, local education surcharge and individual income tax payable in the current year.

### (IV) Social Insurance Subsidy Policies
1.  **Social Insurance Subsidy for Flexible Employment of Unemployed College Graduates After Graduation**: For unemployed local college graduates within 2 years of graduation, whose first employment is flexible employment, have completed personal employment registration in Beijing for more than 30 days, and declare and pay social insurance premiums as a flexible employee, can apply for the social insurance subsidy for flexible employment, with a maximum subsidy period of no more than 24 months.
