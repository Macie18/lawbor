## Employment Support Policies for College Graduates in Tianjin
### (I) Special Talent Subsidy Policies
#### Subsidies Available for Individual Graduates
1.  **One-off Job Hunting and Entrepreneurship Subsidy**: For full-time college graduates in the graduation academic year from general higher education institutions in Tianjin, who meet any of the following conditions (excluding定向 and commissioned training students): families receiving minimum living security for urban and rural residents, poverty-alleviated families, persons in extreme poverty, persons with disabilities, those who received national student loans during the graduation academic year, zero-employment families, and families under the monitoring system for poverty prevention. The subsidy standard is **3,000 yuan per person, limited to one time per person**.
2.  **Employment Internship Subsidy**: Tianjin collects no less than 10,000 employment internship positions every year. Unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation and graduates in the graduation academic year can participate in employment internships for a maximum of 12 months at recognized internship bases. During the internship period, a **living expense subsidy of 2,000 yuan per person per month** is given to the interns. At the same time, a **teaching subsidy of 200 yuan per person per month** and a **personal accident insurance subsidy of 120 yuan per person per year** are given to the internship base.

#### Employment Subsidies for Employing Units
1.  **One-off Job Expansion Subsidy**: Starting from January 1, 2025, various enterprises and social organizations in Tianjin that recruit graduates in the graduation year and unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation, sign a labor contract of more than 1 year, and continuously pay social insurance premiums for more than 3 months in accordance with the law, will be given a one-off job expansion subsidy at the standard of **1,000 yuan per person recruited**.
2.  **Tax and Fee Reduction for Employment Absorption**: For enterprises that recruit college graduates who have been registered as unemployed for more than 6 months at the public employment service agency of the human resources and social security department and hold the *Employment and Entrepreneurship Certificate*, sign a labor contract of more than 1 year with them, and pay social insurance premiums in accordance with the law, from the month of signing the labor contract and paying social insurance, a fixed tax and fee deduction of **7,800 yuan per person per year** is granted within 3 years according to the actual number of recruited personnel. The deduction is applied sequentially to value-added tax, urban maintenance and construction tax, education surcharge, local education surcharge and corporate income tax. The policy is valid until December 31, 2027.

### (II) Social Insurance Subsidy Policies
1.  **Social Insurance and Post Subsidies for Employment Absorption by Small and Micro Enterprises and Other Entities**: Small and micro enterprises, elderly care service enterprises (including private non-enterprise units), employee-based housekeeping enterprises, and individual industrial and commercial households insured in the form of units, which recruit college graduates in the graduation year and unemployed within 2 years of graduation in Tianjin (including graduates from Tianjin universities and non-local graduates with Tianjin household registration from other universities), will be given **social insurance subsidies for a maximum of 3 years and post subsidies for 1 year**.
    - Post subsidy standard: 40% of the minimum wage standard in Tianjin. The post subsidy shall be applied together with the social insurance subsidy after 2 years of enjoying the social insurance subsidy. If the actual salary paid by the enterprise is lower than the post subsidy standard due to absenteeism, personal leave and other reasons of the recruited personnel, the post subsidy shall be paid according to the actual salary.
    - Social insurance subsidy standard: 1,400 yuan per month. If the actual social insurance premium paid by the unit is lower than the subsidy standard, the subsidy shall be paid according to the actual payment amount.
2.  **Social Insurance Subsidy for Flexible Employment of Unemployed College Graduates After Graduation**: Unemployed college graduates within 2 years of graduation, who have achieved flexible employment in Tianjin, completed employment registration, and paid urban employee basic endowment insurance premiums and basic medical insurance premiums as an individual, will be subsidized according to the following standards:
    - For those who pay the basic endowment insurance premiums for enterprise employees: 600 yuan per person per month;
    - For those who pay the basic medical insurance premiums for employees: 200 yuan per person per month;
    - For those who pay both insurances at the same time: 800 yuan per person per month.
    - The subsidy period is calculated from the month of the first application, with a maximum of 2 years. No subsidy will be given for months and insurance types without social insurance payment during the 2 years.
3.  **Social Insurance Security for Employment Internship**: The employment internship base in Tianjin shall purchase personal accident insurance for the interns, and is encouraged to pay social insurance for the interns. If the intern is retained by the internship base after the internship and signs a labor contract, the internship period can be counted as the length of service, and the social insurance relationship transfer shall be handled in accordance with regulations.
