# Summary of Shanghai's Five Social Insurances, Housing Fund and Related Social Security Policies
**Based on the official content of the Social Insurance Premiums Column of the State Taxation Administration Shanghai Municipal Taxation Bureau**
> Note: This summary is fully based on the currently effective policies of Shanghai Municipality. From November 1, 2020, all social insurance premiums for enterprise employees in Shanghai have been uniformly collected by the tax authorities, and all payment-related businesses can be handled through Shanghai Taxation channels.
---
## I. Five Social Insurances and Housing Fund
### (I) Contribution Rate Inquiry (Shanghai Exclusive Standards)

#### 2. Contribution Rates for Flexibly Employed Persons
Flexibly employed persons refer to self-employed persons, individual industrial and commercial households without employees, part-time employees not participating in basic endowment and medical insurance with the employer, and other flexibly employed persons who are at least 16 years old, with men under 60 years old and women under 55 years old, and employed in the city.
- Employee Endowment Insurance: Contribution rate of 20%, fully borne by the individual
- Employee Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance): Contribution rate of 10%, fully borne by the individual
- Optional Insurance: Housing Provident Fund, which can be voluntarily contributed at a rate of 10%-24%, with the full monthly contribution borne by the individual. Those who have contributed for 6 months can enjoy the same provident fund loan benefits as enterprise employees
- Contribution Base: Can be independently selected between the upper and lower limits of the social insurance contribution base of Shanghai in the current year (July 2025 - June 2026: upper limit 37302 RMB/month, lower limit 7460 RMB/month)

#### 3. Official Inquiry Channels
Official website of the State Taxation Administration Shanghai Municipal Taxation Bureau (Social Insurance Premiums Column), official website of Shanghai Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, official website of Shanghai Municipal Healthcare Security Bureau, Shanghai Housing Provident Fund Website.

### (II) Impacts of Payment Interruption
#### 1. Core Impacts of Interruption of the Five Social Insurances
| Insurance | Specific Impacts of Payment Interruption |
| :--- | :--- |
| Endowment Insurance | In accordance with the *Social Insurance Law of the People's Republic of China*, individuals participating in the basic endowment insurance shall receive a monthly basic pension upon reaching the statutory retirement age, provided that the cumulative contribution period is at least 15 years. The cumulative contribution period includes the deemed contribution period, no continuous payment is required, and the contribution years and individual account balance before and after the interruption are combined and accumulated. <br/>Pension calculation follows the principle of "more contributions, more benefits; longer contributions, more benefits". The longer the payment interruption period, the shorter the cumulative contribution years, the less the accumulated amount in the individual account, and the relatively lower the pension benefits after retirement. If the cumulative contribution period is less than 15 years upon reaching the statutory retirement age, the payment shall be extended until the 15-year requirement is met. <br/>Only unpaid social insurance premiums during the period of employment with an employer can be applied for supplementary payment by the employer. For the supplementary payment period within 6 months from the handling time, employment and hiring materials shall be provided; for more than 6 months, additional materials such as archives, wage payment vouchers, and legal documents shall be provided. |
| Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance) | From the month following the payment interruption, the insured shall cease to enjoy the medical insurance pooling reimbursement benefits, while the balance in the individual account can be used normally. If a flexibly employed person's payment is interrupted for more than 3 months, a 6-month benefit waiting period shall be set after the payment is resumed. To apply for maternity benefits for flexibly employed persons, the insured must have made continuous contributions for 12 months before childbirth/abortion; payment interruption will directly affect the application eligibility. Payment interruption will affect the cumulative contribution period of medical insurance, thereby affecting the eligibility for lifetime medical insurance benefits after retirement. |
| Unemployment Insurance | The contribution period is calculated cumulatively. Payment interruption does not affect the accumulated contribution period, but the core requirement for receiving unemployment insurance benefits is a cumulative contribution of at least 1 year before unemployment. |
| Work-related Injury Insurance | If a work-related accident occurs during the period of payment interruption, the work-related injury insurance fund shall not pay the relevant benefits, and all statutory benefits shall be fully borne by the employer. |
| Additional Impacts | There are strict requirements on continuous social insurance contributions for policies such as house purchase, household registration, and residence permit points in Shanghai. Payment interruption will directly affect the application for relevant qualifications. |

#### 2. Core Impacts of Housing Provident Fund Payment Interruption
- To apply for a housing provident fund loan, continuous contributions for at least 6 months before the application (12 months for flexibly employed persons) are required. Payment interruption of 1 month or more (except for seamless renewal) will reset the continuous contribution period to zero, making the applicant ineligible for a provident fund loan;
- The account will be properly sealed during the interruption period, which does not affect the existing account balance. Withdrawal can be processed normally for those who meet specific conditions (e.g., non-local registered persons with the account sealed for more than 6 months);
- For already disbursed provident fund loans, payment interruption does not affect the normal repayment, as long as the loan is repaid in full and on time. Attention should be paid to possible gaps in monthly offset repayment, and the repayment funds should be supplemented in a timely manner.

### (III) Transfer and Continuity Process
#### 1. Inter-provincial/Inter-pooling Area Transfer and Continuity of Social Insurance
Social insurance premiums in Shanghai are uniformly collected by the tax authorities. The core handling entities for transfer and continuity are the social insurance/medical insurance handling agencies, the tax authorities are responsible for the verification and confirmation of payment records. The whole process can be handled online without travel between the two places.
- **Basic Endowment Insurance Transfer**
The insured may use the National Government Affairs Service Platform for Human Resources and Social Security, the National Public Service Platform for Social Insurance, or the mobile application "Palm 12333", select the service "Inquiry on Opened Areas for Online Application of Social Insurance Transfer", to check whether the intended transfer-in location has opened the online application service. For areas with the service opened, the insured shall select the "Social Insurance Transfer Application" service, fill in and submit the transfer application information after successful real-person authentication. The transfer-in handling agency is responsible for accepting the application and conducting background review. If the transfer conditions are verified to be met, the subsequent transfer process will be initiated.
- **Medical Insurance Transfer**
  1. Application Method: The insured/employer may enter the application information online by logging into the medical insurance information platform, or submit the transfer and continuity application offline at the handling agency window with a valid ID/medical security certificate;
  2. Qualification Verification: The handling agency shall conduct qualification verification relying on the medical insurance information platform, accept the application successfully if the conditions are met, and reject the application with a one-time explanation of the reasons if the conditions are not met;
  3. Transfer Handling: The transfer-out and transfer-in handling agencies shall jointly handle the transfer and continuity procedures. The transfer-out location shall generate the *Information Form* and transfer the individual account balance within 10 working days, and the transfer-in location shall check and receive the *Information Form* and individual account amount within 5 working days, and synchronize them to the local medical insurance information platform;
  4. Progress Inquiry: The insured/employer may check the business processing progress through the medical insurance information platform.
- **Unemployment Insurance Transfer**
  1. After stopping the insurance in Shanghai, submit a transfer application to the unemployment insurance handling agency at the transfer-in location;
  2. After acceptance, the transfer-in location shall send a contact letter, the Shanghai handling agency shall transfer the corresponding funds and issue the payment record. For those with a contribution period of less than 1 year, only the relationship shall be transferred without fund transfer;
  3. After the transfer-in location completes the process, the contribution years shall be accumulated and merged, and the unemployment benefit standard shall be verified in accordance with the regulations of the transfer-in location.

#### 2. Transfer and Continuity of Housing Provident Fund
1. **Off-site Transfer into Shanghai**: After opening a provident fund account in Shanghai and making normal contributions, submit an off-site transfer and continuity application through the National Housing Provident Fund Mini Program or Suishenban APP. The Shanghai Provident Fund Center will complete the account merger with the transfer-out location after acceptance;
2. **Transfer from Shanghai to Off-site**: After sealing the provident fund account in Shanghai, submit a transfer application to the provident fund center at the transfer-in location, and handle it in accordance with the process of the transfer-in location. The Shanghai Provident Fund Center will cooperate in the balance transfer and information transfer, with the whole process completed within 11 working days.

### (IV) Usage Guide (Medical Treatment, Provident Fund Loan)
#### 1. Medical Insurance Usage Guide
- **Prerequisite for Benefit Enjoyment**: Normal insured contribution with no benefit waiting period due to payment interruption, then the insured can enjoy Shanghai employee medical insurance benefits. During the period of receiving unemployment insurance benefits, the unemployment insurance fund shall pay the medical insurance premiums on behalf of the insured, with no personal contribution, and the insured shall enjoy medical insurance benefits normally.
- **Outpatient Usage**
  | Insured Category | Annual Deductible | Reimbursement Rate at Primary Hospitals | Reimbursement Rate at Secondary Hospitals | Reimbursement Rate at Tertiary Hospitals |
  | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
  | Working Employees | 500 RMB | 80% | 75% | 70% |
  | Retirees | 300 RMB | 85% | 80% | 75% |
  - Settlement Method: Direct card settlement at designated medical institutions in Shanghai with a social security card/medical insurance electronic voucher, and the individual only pays the self-paid portion. After completing the off-site medical treatment filing, direct settlement can be made at designated medical institutions nationwide.
- **Inpatient Usage**
  | Insured Category | Single Inpatient Deductible | Reimbursement Rate Within the Pooling Fund (Deductible - 650,000 RMB) | Reimbursement Rate for Amount Exceeding the Pooling Limit |
  | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
  | Working Employees | 1500 RMB | 85% | 80% reimbursement by the local supplementary fund |
  | Retirees | 1200 RMB | 92% | 80% reimbursement by the local supplementary fund |
  - Settlement Method: Direct card settlement at designated medical institutions, and nationwide direct settlement after off-site filing.
- **Maternity Insurance Benefits**: Integrated into employee medical insurance. The maternity allowance is calculated as the average monthly wage of the employer in the previous year ÷ 30 × the number of days of maternity leave allowance the employee is entitled to, and paid by the medical insurance fund.

#### 2. Housing Provident Fund Loan Usage Guide
- **Application Prerequisites**
  1. Continuous housing provident fund contributions for at least 6 months before the application, with the account in normal contribution status;
  2. Compliance with Shanghai's housing purchase restriction policies, purchasing owner-occupied housing in the city;
  3. No outstanding provident fund loans, and personal credit meets the requirements.
- **Limit and Interest Rate**
  1. **Basic Maximum Limit**
     | Housing Type | Basic Limit for Single Depositor | Basic Limit for Joint Depositors | Additional Limit for Supplementary Provident Fund |
     | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
     | First Home | 1 million RMB | 2 million RMB | +200,000 RMB for individual, +400,000 RMB for family |
     | Second Home | 800,000 RMB | 1.6 million RMB | +200,000 RMB for individual, +400,000 RMB for family |
     - Additional Increase Rules: Maximum 20% increase for families with multiple children, maximum 15% increase for purchasing green building housing of two-star grade and above, the two policies can be superimposed.
  2. **Loan Interest Rate**
     - First Home: Annual interest rate of 2.35% for 5 years or less, 2.85% for more than 5 years;
     - Second Home: Annual interest rate of 2.775% for 5 years or less, 3.325% for more than 5 years.
- **Down Payment Ratio**
  - First Home: Minimum down payment of 20%;
  - Second improved housing: Minimum down payment of 25%;
  - Second improved housing located in Lingang New Area and the entire territory of 6 administrative districts including Jiading, Qingpu, Songjiang, Fengxian, Baoshan and Jinshan: Minimum down payment of 20%.

## II. Work-related Injury Handling
### (I) Determination Standards and Process
#### 1. Statutory Determination Standards
- **Circumstances That Shall Be Determined as Work-related Injury**
  1. Suffering an accidental injury due to work reasons during working hours and in the workplace;
  2. Suffering an accidental injury from preparatory or finishing work related to the job in the workplace before or after working hours;
  3. Suffering accidental injury such as violence due to performing work duties during working hours and in the workplace;
  4. Suffering from an occupational disease;
  5. Suffering injury or missing in an accident due to work reasons during work-related travel;
  6. Suffering injury from a traffic accident, urban rail transit, passenger ferry, or train accident for which the person is not primarily responsible, on the way to or from work;
  7. Other circumstances that shall be determined as work-related injury in accordance with laws and administrative regulations.
- **Circumstances That Shall Be Deemed as Work-related Injury**
  1. Sudden illness leading to death or death after failed rescue within 48 hours during working hours and at the work post;
  2. Suffering injury in activities to safeguard national interests or public interests such as emergency rescue and disaster relief;
  3. Employees who served in the army, became disabled due to war or duty, obtained a Revolutionary Disabled Soldier Certificate, and have a recurrence of the old injury after joining the employer.
- **Circumstances That Shall Not Be Determined or Deemed as Work-related Injury**: Intentional crime; intoxication or drug use; self-harm or suicide.
- **Supplementary Insurance Coverage**: Employees over the statutory retirement age under 65 years old and interns may be separately covered by work-related injury insurance by the employer, and enjoy work-related injury benefits in accordance with regulations if a work-related injury occurs after insurance participation. Mobile employees of construction projects shall participate in insurance by project and be included in the coverage of work-related injury insurance.

#### 2. Determination and Handling Process
1. **Application Time Limit**: The employer shall submit an application for work-related injury determination to the District Human Resources and Social Security Bureau (HRSSB) at the place of registration of the unit within 30 days from the date of the accidental injury or the date of diagnosis of occupational disease. If the employer fails to apply on time, the employee/close relatives/trade union may directly submit the application within 1 year.
2. **Application Materials**: Work-related injury determination application form, proof of labor relationship, medical diagnosis certificate/occupational disease diagnosis certificate, and relevant proof materials of the accidental injury.
3. **Acceptance and Review**: The District HRSSB shall make an acceptance decision within 15 days, and inform the applicant of the supplements to be made in one go if the materials are incomplete. After acceptance, it may conduct investigation and verification of the accidental injury, and the relevant entities and individuals shall cooperate.
4. **Determination Decision**: A work-related injury determination decision shall be made within 60 days from the date of acceptance, issuing the *Decision on Determination of Work-related Injury* or *Decision on Rejection of Work-related Injury Determination*, which shall be served in writing to the employer and the employee.
5. **Labor Capacity Appraisal**: After the work-related injury is determined, if the employee has a disability affecting labor capacity after the injury is stable, he/she shall apply to the district/county appraisal committee for appraisal of disability grade and self-care ability grade. The appraisal conclusion shall be made within 60 days, and may be extended for 30 days if necessary.

### (II) Calculation of Compensation Items
Compensation items are divided into two categories: **paid by the work-related injury insurance fund** and **paid by the employer**. For those not insured in accordance with the law, all benefits shall be fully borne by the employer in accordance with the standard.

### (III) Dispute Resolution Channels
1. **Work-related Injury Determination Dispute**: If the employee or the employer is dissatisfied with the work-related injury determination decision or the rejection decision, he/she may apply for administrative reconsideration within 60 days from the date of receiving the decision; if dissatisfied with the reconsideration decision, he/she may file an administrative lawsuit within 15 days, or directly file an administrative lawsuit within 6 months from the date of receiving the decision.
2. **Work-related Injury Benefit Payment Dispute**: Disputes between the employee and the employer regarding work-related injury benefits, salary during work suspension period, disability employment allowance, etc., are labor disputes. The employee may apply for labor arbitration to the District Labor and Personnel Dispute Arbitration Committee at the place of registration of the employer or the place of performance of the labor contract within 1 year from the date of the dispute; if dissatisfied with the arbitration award, he/she may file a civil lawsuit with the people's court within 15 days.
3. **Other Disputes**: If dissatisfied with the benefits verified by the work-related injury insurance handling agency, the applicant may apply for administrative reconsideration or file an administrative lawsuit. If the employer fails to pay work-related injury insurance premiums in accordance with the law, a complaint and report may be filed with the Shanghai Municipal Taxation Bureau, which shall order the employer to make supplementary payment within a time limit.

## III. Unemployment Insurance
### (I) Eligibility Judgment for Collection
An applicant may apply for Shanghai unemployment insurance benefits if all of the following conditions are met at the same time:
1. The employer and the applicant have paid unemployment insurance premiums for a cumulative period of at least 1 year before unemployment, and Shanghai is the last payment place;
2. The employment is interrupted not due to the applicant's own will (including expiration of the labor contract, dissolution/dismissal by the employer, termination of the labor contract by the employee due to the employer's illegal employment, etc.);
3. The applicant has completed unemployment registration and has job-seeking requirements.

**Supplementary Rights and Interests**: During the period of receiving unemployment insurance benefits, the unemployment insurance fund shall pay the employee basic medical insurance (including maternity insurance) premiums on behalf of the insured, with no personal contribution, and the insured shall enjoy employee medical insurance benefits normally. Free job introduction, vocational training and other employment services are available.

**Circumstances Ineligible for Collection**: Re-employment; enlistment in active military service; emigration abroad; enjoying basic endowment insurance benefits; being imprisoned and executed for a crime or undergoing reeducation through labor; refusing to accept an appropriate job or training introduced by the public employment service agency without justifiable reasons.
---
### (II) Amount Calculation
#### 1. Payment Standards (effective from July 1, 2025)
- Payment standard for the 1st-12th months: 2305 RMB/month (90% of the minimum wage standard in Shanghai);
- Payment standard for the 13th-24th months: 1844 RMB/month (calculated at 80% of the standard for the first 12 months);
- Extended payment standard: 1650 RMB/month for those who are still unemployed after 24 months of collection and meet the extension conditions.

#### 2. Verification of Collection Period
- Cumulative payment of 1 year or more but less than 2 years: collection period of 2 months;
- Cumulative payment of 2 years or more but less than 3 years: collection period of 4 months;
- For each additional year of cumulative unemployment insurance contribution, the period increases by 2 months;
- Cumulative payment of 3 years or more but less than 5 years: maximum collection period of 12 months;
- Cumulative payment of 5 years or more but less than 10 years: maximum collection period of 18 months;
- Cumulative payment of 10 years or more: maximum collection period of 24 months.

**Supplementary Rules**: If re-employment leads to unemployment again, the payment period shall be recalculated, and the uncollected period from the previous time may be combined for calculation, with the maximum combined period not exceeding 24 months.
---
### (III) Application Process
1. **Online Application** (full online processing, no offline materials required): Search for "Unemployment Insurance Benefit Application" through Suishenban APP/Mini Program, "Palm 12333" APP, or the one-stop service column on the official website of Shanghai Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, check personal information and submit the application.
2. **Offline Application**: Bring your ID card and social security card to the District Employment Promotion Center or sub-district community affairs service center at the place of household registration/residence to complete the unemployment registration and application procedures on site.
3. **Review and Payment**: The handling agency shall complete the review and inform the result within 5 working days. If the application is approved, the unemployment insurance benefits shall be calculated from the date of unemployment registration and paid monthly to the applicant's social security card bank account or designated bank account. Normal payment can be made by completing the monthly employment status confirmation during the collection period.
