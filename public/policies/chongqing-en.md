# Summary of Chongqing's Five Social Insurances, Housing Fund and Related Social Security Policies
**Based on the official content of the Social Insurance Premiums Column of the State Taxation Administration Chongqing Municipal Taxation Bureau**
> Note: This summary is fully based on the currently effective policies of Chongqing Municipality. From November 1, 2020, all social insurance premiums for enterprise employees in Chongqing have been uniformly collected by the tax authorities, and all payment-related businesses can be handled through Chongqing Taxation channels.
---
## I. Five Social Insurances and Housing Fund
### (I) Contribution Rate Inquiry (Chongqing Exclusive Standards)
#### 1. Contribution Rates for Enterprise Employees
| Type of Insurance | Employer Contribution Rate | Employee Contribution Rate | Core Policy Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Basic Endowment Insurance | 16% | 8% | Unified standard for the whole city, uniformly collected by the tax authorities |
| Basic Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance) | 8.5% (8% basic medical insurance + 0.5% maternity insurance) | 2% | Maternity insurance is integrated with employee medical insurance, with no separate personal contribution item. Large-sum medical mutual aid insurance shall be paid simultaneously in accordance with regulations |
| Unemployment Insurance | 0.50% | 0.50% | Unified standard for the whole city, with cumulative calculation of contribution years. The phased fee reduction policy is extended to December 31, 2026 |
| Work-related Injury Insurance | 0.2%-1.9% (benchmark rates for 8 industry risk levels) | 0% | Divided into 8 tiers according to industry risk, corresponding to benchmark rates of 0.2%, 0.4%, 0.7%, 0.9%, 1.1%, 1.3%, 1.6%, 1.9%. Construction projects shall participate in insurance and pay contributions separately by project, with no employee contribution |
| Housing Provident Fund (Statutory) | 5%-12% (same proportion as employee) | 5%-12% (same proportion as employer) | Contributing entities may independently choose the contribution rate within the 5%-12% range, and the employer and employee contribution rates must be consistent |

#### 2. Official Inquiry Channels
Official website of the State Taxation Administration Chongqing Municipal Taxation Bureau (Social Insurance Premiums Column), official website of Chongqing Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, official website of Chongqing Municipal Healthcare Security Bureau, official website of Chongqing Municipal Housing Provident Fund Management Center.

### (II) Impacts of Payment Interruption
#### 1. Core Impacts of Interruption of the Five Social Insurances
| Insurance | Specific Impacts of Payment Interruption |
| :--- | :--- |
| Endowment Insurance | In accordance with the *Social Insurance Law of the People's Republic of China*: Individuals participating in the basic endowment insurance shall receive a monthly basic pension upon reaching the statutory retirement age, provided that the cumulative contribution period is at least 15 years. Interruption of social insurance contributions does not affect the cumulative calculation of the endowment insurance contribution period; the contribution years before and after the interruption are calculated cumulatively, and the balance in the endowment insurance individual account continues to accumulate. <br/>Pension calculation follows the principle of "more contributions, more benefits; longer contributions, more benefits". The longer the payment interruption period, the shorter the cumulative contribution years, the relatively less the accumulated amount in the individual account, and the lower the pension received after retirement. If the cumulative contribution period is less than 15 years upon reaching the statutory retirement age, the payment shall be extended until the 15-year requirement is met in accordance with regulations. <br/>Only unpaid social insurance premiums during the period of employment with an employer can be applied for supplementary payment by the employer; individuals shall not arbitrarily make supplementary payment for historical arrears, and supplementary payment shall provide materials such as labor relationship and wage payment vouchers in accordance with requirements. |
| Medical Insurance (including Maternity Insurance) | From the month following the payment interruption, the insured shall cease to enjoy the medical insurance pooling reimbursement benefits, while the balance in the individual account can be used normally. If a flexibly employed person's payment is interrupted for more than 3 months, a 6-month benefit waiting period shall be set after the payment is resumed. To apply for maternity allowance for enterprise employees, the insured must have made continuous contributions for 6 months before childbirth; payment interruption will directly affect the application eligibility. Payment interruption will affect the cumulative contribution period of medical insurance. Chongqing stipulates that the cumulative contribution period of employee medical insurance must be at least 30 years for males and 25 years for females, including at least 10 years of actual payment in Chongqing, to enjoy lifetime medical insurance benefits after retirement. |
| Unemployment Insurance | The contribution period is calculated cumulatively. Payment interruption does not affect the accumulated contribution period, but the core requirement for receiving unemployment insurance benefits is a cumulative contribution of at least 1 year before unemployment. |
| Work-related Injury Insurance | If a work-related accident occurs during the period of payment interruption, the work-related injury insurance fund shall not pay the relevant benefits, and all statutory benefits shall be fully borne by the employer. |
| Additional Impacts | There are strict requirements on continuous social insurance contributions for policies such as house purchase, household registration, residence permit points, and talent subsidies in Chongqing. Payment interruption will directly affect the application for relevant qualifications. |

#### 2. Core Impacts of Housing Provident Fund Payment Interruption
- To apply for a housing provident fund loan, continuous contributions for at least 6 months are required. Payment interruption of 1 month or more will reset the continuous contribution period to zero, making the applicant ineligible for a provident fund loan (except for seamless renewal);
- The account will be properly sealed during the interruption period, which does not affect the existing account balance. Withdrawal can be processed normally if the withdrawal conditions are met;
- For already disbursed provident fund loans, payment interruption does not affect the normal repayment, as long as the loan is repaid in full and on time. Attention should be paid to possible gaps in monthly offset repayment, and the funds in the repayment account should be supplemented in a timely manner.

### (III) Transfer and Continuity Process
#### 1. Inter-provincial/Inter-pooling Area Transfer and Continuity of Social Insurance
Social insurance premiums in Chongqing are uniformly collected by the tax authorities. The core handling entities for transfer and continuity are the social insurance/medical insurance handling agencies, the tax authorities are responsible for the verification and confirmation of payment records. The whole process can be handled online without travel between the two places.
- **Basic Endowment Insurance Transfer**
The insured may select the "Social Insurance Transfer Application" service through the National Government Affairs Service Platform for Human Resources and Social Security, National Public Service Platform for Social Insurance, "Palm 12333" mobile application, or Yukuaiban APP, fill in and submit the transfer application information after successful real-person authentication. The transfer-in handling agency is responsible for accepting the application and conducting background review. If the transfer conditions are verified to be met, the subsequent transfer process will be initiated.
- **Medical Insurance Transfer**
The insured may submit a transfer application online through the National Medical Insurance Service Platform APP, Yukuaiban APP, or the official website of Chongqing Municipal Healthcare Security Bureau, or apply offline at the window of the medical insurance handling agency with a valid ID. The handling agency shall conduct qualification verification relying on the medical insurance information platform, accept the application successfully if the conditions are met. The transfer-out and transfer-in handling agencies shall jointly handle the transfer and continuity procedures, transfer the individual account balance, and synchronize the contribution years. The insured may check the processing progress through the medical insurance information platform.
- **Unemployment Insurance Transfer**
1. After stopping the insurance in Chongqing, submit a transfer application to the unemployment insurance handling agency at the transfer-in location;
2. After acceptance, the transfer-in location shall send a contact letter, the Chongqing handling agency shall transfer the corresponding funds and issue the payment record. For those with a contribution period of less than 1 year, only the relationship shall be transferred without fund transfer;
3. After the transfer-in location completes the process, the contribution years shall be accumulated and merged, and the unemployment benefit standard shall be verified in accordance with the regulations of the transfer-in location.

#### 2. Transfer and Continuity of Housing Provident Fund
1. **Off-site Transfer into Chongqing**: After opening a provident fund account in Chongqing and making stable contributions for more than 6 months, submit an off-site transfer and continuity application through the National Housing Provident Fund Mini Program, Yukuaiban APP, or the official website of Chongqing Municipal Housing Provident Fund Management Center. The Chongqing Provident Fund Center will complete the account merger with the transfer-out location after acceptance;
2. **Transfer from Chongqing to Off-site**: After sealing the provident fund account in Chongqing, submit a transfer application to the provident fund center at the transfer-in location, and handle it in accordance with the process of the transfer-in location. The Chongqing Provident Fund Center will cooperate in the balance transfer and information transfer, with the whole process completed within 6-10 working days.

### (IV) Usage Guide (Medical Treatment, Provident Fund Loan)
#### 1. Medical Insurance Usage Guide
- **Prerequisite for Benefit Enjoyment**: Normal insured contribution with no benefit waiting period due to payment interruption, then the insured can enjoy Chongqing employee medical insurance benefits. During the period of receiving unemployment insurance benefits, the unemployment insurance fund shall pay the employee basic medical insurance (including maternity insurance) premiums on behalf of the insured, with no personal contribution, and the insured shall enjoy employee medical insurance benefits normally.
- **Outpatient Reimbursement**
| Item | Working Insured Persons | Retired Insured Persons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Deductible Standard | 200 RMB/year | 100 RMB/year |
| Reimbursement Rate | 60% for secondary and lower medical institutions, 50% for tertiary medical institutions | 70% for secondary and lower medical institutions, 60% for tertiary medical institutions |
| Reimbursement Limit | Combined with inpatient calculation, maximum payment of 47,000 RMB/year from the pooling fund, maximum payment of 500,000 RMB/year from the large-sum fund, total 547,000 RMB/year | Same as working insured persons |
- **Inpatient Usage**
| Item | Primary Hospital | Secondary Hospital | Tertiary Hospital |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Deductible Standard | 200 RMB/time | 440 RMB/time | 880 RMB/time |
| Reimbursement Rate for Working Insured | 90% | 87% | 85% |
| Reimbursement Rate for Retired Insured | 95% | 95% | 95% |
| Multiple Hospitalization Rules | For each additional hospitalization, the deductible is reduced by 10%, with a minimum of 100 RMB, 260 RMB, and 620 RMB respectively | - | - |
- **Maternity Insurance Benefits**: Integrated into employee medical insurance. The maternity allowance is calculated as the average monthly wage of the employer in the previous year ÷ 30 × the number of statutory maternity leave days, and paid by the medical insurance fund. Maternity medical expenses in line with the policy are subject to quota/fixed amount reimbursement.

#### 2. Housing Provident Fund Loan Usage Guide
- **Application Prerequisites**
1. Continuous housing provident fund contributions for at least 6 months before the application, with the account in normal contribution status;
2. Compliance with Chongqing's housing purchase restriction policies, purchasing owner-occupied housing in the city;
3. No outstanding provident fund loans, and personal credit meets the loan requirements.
- **Limit and Interest Rate**
1. **Basic Maximum Limit**: The individual loan limit for housing provident fund personal housing loans is 25 times the balance of the housing provident fund account, with a maximum individual limit of 800,000 RMB, and a maximum individual limit of 1 million RMB for families with multiple children. The loan limit for couples participating in the loan is the sum of the individual loan limits of the depositor and his/her spouse, and the total amount of a single housing provident fund loan shall not exceed 1.2 million RMB, and the total loan amount for families with multiple children shall not exceed 1.6 million RMB.
2. **Loan Interest Rate**: The interest rate of housing provident fund personal housing loans shall be implemented in accordance with the regulations of the People's Bank of China, and adjusted accordingly with the interest rate adjustment of the People's Bank of China. At present, for families purchasing the first home, the annual interest rate is 2.1% for loans with a term of 5 years or less, and 2.6% for loans with a term of more than 5 years; for families purchasing the second home, the annual interest rate is 2.525% for loans with a term of 5 years or less, and 3.075% for loans with a term of more than 5 years.
3. **Down Payment Ratio**: For applying for provident fund loans for the first and second homes, the minimum down payment ratio is unified at 20%.

## II. Work-related Injury Handling
### (I) Determination Standards and Process
#### 1. Statutory Determination Standards
- **Circumstances That Shall Be Determined as Work-related Injury**
1. Suffering an accidental injury due to work reasons during working hours and in the workplace;
2. Suffering an accidental injury from preparatory or finishing work related to the job in the workplace before or after working hours;
3. Suffering accidental injury such as violence due to performing work duties during working hours and in the workplace;
4. Suffering from an occupational disease;
5. Suffering injury or missing in an accident due to work reasons during work-related travel;
6. Suffering injury from a traffic accident, urban rail transit, passenger ferry, or train accident for which the person is not primarily responsible, on the way to or from work;
7. Other circumstances that shall be determined as work-related injury in accordance with laws and administrative regulations.
- **Circumstances That Shall Be Deemed as Work-related Injury**
1. Sudden illness leading to death or death after failed rescue within 48 hours during working hours and at the work post;
2. Suffering injury in activities to safeguard national interests or public interests such as emergency rescue and disaster relief;
3. Employees who served in the army, became disabled due to war or duty, obtained a Revolutionary Disabled Soldier Certificate, and have a recurrence of the old injury after joining the employer.
- **Circumstances That Shall Not Be Determined or Deemed as Work-related Injury**: Intentional crime; intoxication or drug use; self-harm or suicide.
- **Supplementary Insurance Coverage**: Employees over the statutory retirement age under 65 years old and student interns may be separately covered by work-related injury insurance by the employer, and enjoy work-related injury benefits in accordance with regulations if a work-related injury occurs after insurance participation. Mobile employees of construction projects shall participate in insurance by project and be included in the coverage of work-related injury insurance.

#### 2. Determination and Handling Process
1. **Application Time Limit**: The employer shall submit an application for work-related injury determination to the District Human Resources and Social Security Bureau (HRSSB) at the place of registration of the unit within 30 days from the date of the accidental injury or the date of diagnosis of occupational disease. If the employer fails to apply on time, the employee/close relatives/trade union may directly submit the application within 1 year.
2. **Application Materials**: Work-related injury determination application form, proof of labor relationship, medical diagnosis certificate/occupational disease diagnosis certificate, and relevant proof materials of the accidental injury.
3. **Acceptance and Review**: The District HRSSB shall make an acceptance decision within 15 days, and inform the applicant of the supplements to be made in one go if the materials are incomplete. After acceptance, it may conduct investigation and verification of the accidental injury, and the relevant entities and individuals shall cooperate.
4. **Determination Decision**: A work-related injury determination decision shall be made within 60 days from the date of acceptance, issuing the *Decision on Determination of Work-related Injury* or *Decision on Rejection of Work-related Injury Determination*, which shall be served in writing to the employer and the employee.
5. **Labor Capacity Appraisal**: After the work-related injury is determined, if the employee has a disability affecting labor capacity after the injury is stable, he/she shall apply to the Chongqing Municipal/District/County Labor Capacity Appraisal Committee for appraisal of disability grade and self-care ability grade. The appraisal conclusion shall be made within 60 days, and may be extended for 30 days if necessary.

### (II) Calculation of Compensation Items
Compensation items are divided into two categories: **paid by the work-related injury insurance fund** and **paid by the employer**. For those not insured in accordance with the law, all benefits shall be fully borne by the employer in accordance with the standard.

#### 1. Items Paid by the Work-related Injury Insurance Fund and Calculation Standards
| Compensation Item | Calculation Standard |
| :--- | :--- |
| Work-related Injury Medical/Rehabilitation Expenses | Full reimbursement for expenses in line with the work-related injury insurance diagnosis and treatment, medicine, and inpatient service standards |
| Inpatient Food Allowance | 8 RMB per day for inpatient treatment in the city, 50 RMB per day fixed amount for inpatient treatment outside the city with approval |
| Transportation, Accommodation and Food Expenses for Off-site Medical Treatment | For off-site medical treatment approved, transportation expenses are reimbursed according to actual public transport tickets, accommodation and food expenses: on-the-way food allowance of 50 RMB per day, accommodation fee not exceeding 100 RMB per day |
| Disability Assistive Device Fees | Full reimbursement for those in line with national and Chongqing standards |
| Life Care Fees | After appraisal and confirmation, calculated based on the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Chongqing: 50% for total inability to self-care, 40% for most inability to self-care, 30% for partial inability to self-care |
| Lump-sum Disability Benefit | Calculated as the average monthly contributory wage of the 12 months before the work-related injury × corresponding number of months: Level 1: 27 months, Level 2: 25 months, Level 3: 23 months, Level 4: 21 months, Level 5: 18 months, Level 6: 16 months, Level 7: 13 months, Level 8: 11 months, Level 9: 9 months, Level 10: 7 months |
| Disability Allowance for Level 1 to Level 4 | Paid monthly, calculated as a proportion of the employee's wage: Level 1: 90%, Level 2: 85%, Level 3: 80%, Level 4: 75%. If the actual amount of the disability allowance is lower than the minimum wage standard in Chongqing, the difference shall be made up by the work-related injury insurance fund. |
| Lump-sum Work-related Injury Medical Allowance | Paid upon dissolution/termination of labor relationship, calculated as the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Chongqing × corresponding number of months: Level 5: 12 months, Level 6: 10 months, Level 7: 8 months, Level 8: 6 months, Level 9: 4 months, Level 10: 2 months |
| Funeral Allowance (Fatal Work-related Injury) | Calculated as the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Chongqing × 6 months |
| Bereavement Pension for Dependent Relatives (Fatal Work-related Injury) | Calculated as a proportion of the employee's wage before death: 40% per month for the spouse, 30% per month for each other dependent relative, with an additional 10% per month for widowed elderly or orphans on the basis of the above standards. The total amount shall not exceed the employee's wage before death. |
| Lump-sum Fatal Work-related Injury Benefit (Fatal Work-related Injury) | Calculated as the per capita disposable income of urban residents nationwide in the previous year × 20 times |

#### 2. Items Paid by the Employer and Calculation Standards
| Compensation Item | Calculation Standard |
| :--- | :--- |
| Salary During Work Suspension Period | During the work suspension period, the original wage and welfare benefits shall remain unchanged and paid monthly. The period shall generally not exceed 12 months, and may be extended with approval for serious injuries, but the extension shall not exceed 12 months. |
| Disability Allowance for Level 5 and Level 6 | For those who retain the labor relationship and are difficult to arrange work, paid monthly, calculated as a proportion of the employee's wage: Level 5: 70%, Level 6: 60%. If it is lower than the minimum wage standard in Chongqing, the employer shall make up the difference. |
| Lump-sum Disability Employment Allowance | Paid upon dissolution/termination of labor relationship, calculated as the average monthly employee wage of the previous year in Chongqing × corresponding number of months: Level 5: 60 months, Level 6: 48 months, Level 7: 15 months, Level 8: 12 months, Level 9: 9 months, Level 10: 6 months. For those less than 10 years away from the statutory retirement age, it shall be reduced by 10% for each year reduction, and 10% shall be paid for those less than 1 year away. |
| Life Care Fees During Work Suspension Period | For injured employees who are unable to take care of themselves and need nursing during the work suspension period, the employer shall be responsible for the nursing. |

### (III) Dispute Resolution Channels
1. **Work-related Injury Determination Dispute**: If the employee or the employer is dissatisfied with the work-related injury determination decision or the rejection decision, he/she may apply for administrative reconsideration within 60 days from the date of receiving the decision; if dissatisfied with the reconsideration decision, he/she may file an administrative lawsuit within 15 days, or directly file an administrative lawsuit within 6 months from the date of receiving the decision.
2. **Work-related Injury Benefit Payment Dispute**: Disputes between the employee and the employer regarding work-related injury benefits, salary during work suspension period, disability employment allowance, etc., are labor disputes. The employee may apply for labor arbitration to the District Labor and Personnel Dispute Arbitration Committee at the place of registration of the employer or the place of performance of the labor contract within 1 year from the date of the dispute; if dissatisfied with the arbitration award, he/she may file a civil lawsuit with the people's court within 15 days.
3. **Other Disputes**: If dissatisfied with the benefits verified by the work-related injury insurance handling agency, the applicant may apply for administrative reconsideration or file an administrative lawsuit. If the employer fails to pay work-related injury insurance premiums in accordance with the law, a complaint and report may be filed with the State Taxation Administration Chongqing Municipal Taxation Bureau, which shall order the employer to make supplementary payment within a time limit.

## III. Unemployment Insurance
### (I) Eligibility Judgment for Collection
An applicant may apply for Chongqing unemployment insurance benefits if all of the following conditions are met at the same time:
1. The employer and the applicant have paid unemployment insurance premiums for a cumulative period of at least 1 year before unemployment;
2. The employment is interrupted not due to the applicant's own will (including expiration of the labor contract, dissolution/dismissal by the employer, termination of the labor contract by the employee due to the employer's illegal employment, etc.);
3. The applicant has completed unemployment registration and has job-seeking requirements.

**Supplementary Rights and Interests**: During the period of receiving unemployment insurance benefits, the unemployment insurance fund shall pay the employee basic medical insurance (including maternity insurance) premiums on behalf of the insured, with no personal contribution, and the insured shall enjoy employee medical insurance benefits normally. Free job introduction, vocational training and other employment services are available.

**Circumstances Ineligible for Collection**: Re-employment; enlistment in active military service; emigration abroad; enjoying basic endowment insurance benefits; being imprisoned and executed for a crime; refusing to accept an appropriate job or training introduced by the public employment service agency without justifiable reasons.
---
### (II) Amount Calculation
#### 1. Payment Standards (effective from January 1, 2025)
- Unified standard for the whole city (except 4 key assistance counties): Paid at 80% of the highest minimum wage in the city (2330 RMB/month), i.e. **1864 RMB/month**, with no grading or difference between the 13th-24th months;
- 4 national key counties for rural revitalization (Chengkou County, Wuxi County, Youyang County, Pengshui County): Paid at 90% of the highest minimum wage, i.e. **2097 RMB/month**.

#### 2. Verification of Collection Period
- Cumulative payment of 1 year or more but less than 2 years: collection period of 3 months;
- Cumulative payment of 2 years or more but less than 3 years: collection period of 6 months;
- Cumulative payment of 3 years or more but less than 4 years: collection period of 9 months;
- Cumulative payment of 4 years or more but less than 5 years: collection period of 12 months;
- Cumulative payment of 5 years or more but less than 7 years: collection period of 15 months;
- For cumulative payment of 7 years or more, the collection period increases by 1 month for each additional year, up to a maximum of 24 months.

**Supplementary Rules**: If re-employment leads to unemployment again, the payment period shall be recalculated, and the uncollected period from the previous time may be combined for calculation, with the maximum combined period not exceeding 24 months.
---
### (III) Application Process
1. **Online Application** (full online processing, no offline materials required): Unemployed persons may apply for unemployment benefits through the "Yukuaiban" APP, "Chongqing Human Resources and Social Security" APP, "Yuzhipin" Mini Program and other channels.
2. **Offline Application**: Bring your ID card or social security card to the District Employment Service Administration Bureau or sub-district community affairs service center at the place of household registration to complete the unemployment registration and application procedures on site (unemployed persons with non-local household registration shall apply to the unemployment insurance handling agency of the insured district/county in the city).
3. **Review and Payment**: The handling agency shall complete the review and inform the result within 7 working days. If the application is approved, the unemployment insurance benefits shall be calculated from the date of unemployment registration and paid monthly to the applicant's social security card bank account or designated bank account. Normal payment can be made by completing the monthly employment status confirmation during the collection period.
